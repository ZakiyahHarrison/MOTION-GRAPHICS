Welcome to PlantBox

Grow beautiful houseplants!

The Plantbox Newsletter is all about helping you find success in growing beautiful, thriving houseplants. If you love plants, want to know more, or are just looking to keep your houseplant plant alive, you’re in the right place!

What PlantBox is About

We started PlantBox back in 2011 because we want to share our love for houseplants. Sharing knowledge about plants brings PlantBox so much joy, and we're thrilled to share with you. At PlantBox, you’ll find practical tips, tools, and advice that will help you find success with your plants, no matter your situation. On the blog, we cover everything from common myths to best practices that help you nurture and grow healthy, thriving houseplants.
